Learning

This site is part of my journey as I change careers and move in a different direction as a Web designer and Programmer - as part of this I am completing a weekly blog on my learning progress and sharing my long and short term learning plans, along with what courses I am undertaking and what I thought of them at the time. I am hoping this may be of some use to those who follow a similar path.

Designing

As part of this journey I will be completing a number of design projects for clients that I will be sharing on this site with how or what technologies I used to complete them. Along with many other design ideas for applications to make life easier, if you see any you would like to collaborrate on be sure to use the contact section.

Solving

My passion for this stems from being frustrated that the apps I use never seem to do everything I want them too, also something missing or not quite right. I plan to resolve this by creating my own apps for a variety of uses, some to cover areas of my old work as a project manager, some to help out wiht some of my other passions in life. Solving day to day problems will be an ongoing feature of the work on this site along with hopefully some great website designs and ideas. I'm also looking for projects to collaborate on and learn from others.

About me

Although this site isn't my portfolio site, a little about me. I have worked with a major blue chip company for the last 25 years in many roles, marketing, contracts, account management and for the last 10 years as a project manager and programmes manager tackling many interesting projects. I am hoping that many of my current skills will have a degree of transferability to this new career plan and I'm grateful to be in a position to have this chance to test that out.

In my spare time, i'm in the final months of a BA in Economics with the open university here in the UK, I also enjoy, cycling, swimming and running and I am planning to do Ironman Barcelona again in 2022 should we be allowed to get back to some form of normality.

Once I have a few projects under my belt I will link to my portfolio here, until then the rest of this site is what I'm working on, feel free to comment on the forums or using the contact section below.
